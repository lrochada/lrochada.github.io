## Courses (Centrale Nantes)

I teach mathematics and computer science within the **Mathématiques–Informatique–Biologie** department, with an emphasis on **numerical methods** and **scientific programming**.

**Main topics**
- Numerical analysis for PDEs (FEM/FDM/FVM), interpolation/integration, and optimisation (with/without constraints)  
- Linear algebra; probability & statistics  
- Algorithms & data structures  
- Scientific computing and programming (C++, Python)  
- Quantum computing; AI and decision support  
- Research methodology (soft skills)

## Teaching practices

I use hands-on formats (labs, mini-hackathons, notebooks) and digital resources (Moodle, reusable quizzes, Jupyter notebooks). I also contributed to the MOOC **“Dopez vos calculs”** on supercomputing performance.

## Programme leadership

Since 2020, I have co-directed the international programme **BBA Data, AI & Management** (Centrale Nantes × Audencia × Centrale Casablanca). In 2024, I coordinated the reaccreditation dossier, which obtained a favourable decision (accreditation until 2029).
